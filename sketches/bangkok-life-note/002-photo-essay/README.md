## Variant: Photo Essay

### Design stance
Let the photographs carry more of the narrative, binding each photo sequence closely to the paragraph that explains it.

### Key choices
- Layout: Wider scene images, short text passages, and section breaks that feel like photo essay chapters.
- Typography: Still readable and restrained, but with stronger section spacing.
- Color: Plain warm white with quiet ink color and thin separators.
- Interaction: Simple hover and text links only.
- Crocodile contrast: Stacked narrative sequence: mall scene first, then the crocodile detail as a smaller reversal image.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: Making photo/story relationships explicit and avoiding mismatched equal grids.
- Weak at: Slightly less intimate than the quiet journal option.

### Best for
- A travel note where the reader should feel the route through scenes before returning to reflective prose.
