## Variant: Editorial Rhythm

### Design stance
Treat the article like a restrained travel magazine feature: prose first, images inserted with changing rhythm and hierarchy.

### Key choices
- Layout: Narrow text column with selective wider image moments.
- Typography: Large quiet title, 18px body, generous line-height, no decorative display font.
- Color: Warm off-white page, charcoal text, muted blue-gray rule accents.
- Interaction: Links and figures have subtle hover states only.
- Crocodile contrast: Non-symmetric editorial split with the mall as the larger scene and the crocodile as a smaller detail.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: Long-form readability, image hierarchy, fixing the crocodile/mall imbalance.
- Weak at: Less dramatic than a photo-led design.

### Best for
- A personal travel note that should feel edited, calm, and more magazine-like without becoming a template.
