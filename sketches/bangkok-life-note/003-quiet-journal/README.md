## Variant: Quiet Journal

### Design stance
Make the article feel like a private travel notebook with careful photo placement rather than a designed feature spread.

### Key choices
- Layout: Mostly single column, smaller images, quiet spacing, and no large gallery moments.
- Typography: Comfortable Chinese reading size with restrained headings.
- Color: Neutral page, soft rules, no strong decorative surface.
- Interaction: Minimal hover and anchor behavior only.
- Crocodile contrast: Inset-like sequence without overlap: mall as the scene, crocodile as a smaller detail after it.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: Mobile stability, intimacy, and natural reading flow.
- Weak at: Less visual punch on desktop.

### Best for
- Readers who value the travel diary voice more than photographic spectacle.
