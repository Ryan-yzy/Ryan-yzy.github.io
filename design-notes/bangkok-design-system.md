# Bangkok Life Note Design System

## Design Temperament

- quiet editorial
- personal travel journal
- restrained
- observational
- image-aware
- warm but not decorative

## Principles

- The prose is the center of the page; images act as evidence and rhythm, not as a gallery wall.
- Image size follows content role. A scene image can be larger; a detail image should stay smaller.
- Paired images need a shared narrative reason and a visible hierarchy.
- Different aspect ratios should not be forced into equal-width or equal-height cells.
- Use width changes, whitespace, and thin rules for rhythm rather than cards, shadows, or decorative effects.
- Mobile comes first: every image sequence must collapse into a natural reading order.
- Keep the academic site's restraint, but give this article a more personal editorial cadence.

## Type Scale

Desktop:

- Body: 18px
- Body line-height: 1.9-2.0
- Caption: 13.5-14px
- H2: 25-28px
- Page title: 38-44px

Mobile:

- Body: 17px
- Body line-height: 1.85-1.95
- Caption: 13px
- H2: 22-24px
- Page title: 29-33px

## Reading Width

- Text column: 680-720px
- Ordinary landscape image: up to 760px
- Portrait image: 360-460px
- Special gallery: up to 820px
- Mobile side padding: about 18px

These values may be adjusted slightly in production after rendering checks.

## Selected Production Direction

The production version should start from `001-editorial-rhythm`, with the mobile restraint of `003-quiet-journal`.

This combination keeps the article readable, avoids over-designed cards, and solves the crocodile/mall section with visible hierarchy instead of a mechanical two-column grid.
